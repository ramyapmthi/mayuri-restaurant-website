Our Story

A Local Favorite for Family Dining in Nellore

Mayuri Family Restaurant is known for its delicious food, warm hospitality, and comfortable dining environment. Located in the heart of Nellore, the restaurant has become a popular spot for families, friends, and food lovers looking to enjoy authentic Indian dishes and flavorful biryanis.

Whether you're planning a family dinner, a casual outing with friends, or simply craving great food, Mayuri offers a dining experience that keeps guests coming back.
